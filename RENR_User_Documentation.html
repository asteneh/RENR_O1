1. App Overview

RENR is a mobile marketplace platform built for the machinery, vehicle, and equipment industry. It connects Buyers, Sellers, Lessors (Akeray), Lessees (Tekeray), Operators, and Employers in a single ecosystem.

What You Can Do

Feature Description
Buy / Rent Browse and purchase machinery, vehicles, and equipment or rent them
Sell / Lease Post your own items for sale or lease with a guided wizard
Jobs Find operator jobs or post job openings
Supplier Requests Post and respond to supply/demand requests
Messaging Chat with sellers, buyers, and operators in real-time
Reviews Rate and review products to help the community
Favorites Save products you are interested in for quick access later

2. Getting Started

2.1 Creating an Account (Registration)

To use the full features of RENR, you need to create an account. Follow these steps:

Step 1 — Personal Information

  1. Open the app and tap "Register" on the login screen.
  2. Enter your First Name and Last Name.
  3. Select your Country by tapping the flag icon.
  4. Enter your Phone Number (primary login).
  5. Enter your Email Address (optional).
  6. Create and confirm your Password.
  7. Select your Role:
    Role Description
    Akeray (Lessor) Own equipment and want to lease it out
    Buyer Want to purchase machinery or vehicles
    Seller Have items to sell on the platform
    Tekeray (Lessee) Want to rent/lease equipment
    Operator Machine operator looking for work
    Employer Want to hire operators
  8. Check the agreement box and tap "Next Step".

Step 2 — Supplier Assets (Sellers & Lessors Only)

  1. Select Machinery Type and Brands.
  2. Tap "Add to List".
  3. Upload Legal Documents (images).
  4. Tap "Next: Membership".

Step 3 — Membership Selection

  1. Choose a membership plan based on your role.
    👷 Operator Note: Operators have an option "Post Through Gadal". If selected, you don't need to choose a membership plan.
  2. Select from Basic, Gold, or Premium plans (details below).
  3. Tap "Complete Registration" to finish.

2.2 Signing In

  1. On the Sign In screen, enter your registered Phone Number.
  2. Enter your Password. You can tap the eye icon to toggle visibility.
  3. Tap "Sign In".
  4. Upon success, you will see a "Welcome back!" notification and be taken to the Home screen.
ℹ️ Not Verified? If your account is not yet verified, a red banner will appear with a "Verify" button. Tap it to receive an OTP and complete verification.

2.3 Password Recovery

  1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?".
  2. Enter your registered phone number.
  3. You will receive an OTP code via SMS.
  4. Enter the OTP on the verification screen.
  5. Create and confirm your new password on the Reset Password screen.

2.4 Phone Verification (OTP)

After registration or when signing in with an unverified account, you will be taken to the Verify Phone screen. Enter the OTP code sent to your phone number to activate your account.

3. App Navigation

The app uses a bottom tab bar with five main sections:

Tab Icon What It Does

Jobs 💼 Find jobs, view operator listings, or post jobs
Post ✏️ Create a new listing (machinery, vehicle)

4. Home Screen

The Home screen is your starting point. It features:

Search Bar

Tap the search bar at the top to quickly find products by keyword. Tap the filter icon to access advanced filters.

Action Buttons

Quick-access buttons at the top let you jump to Cart, Notifications, and Messages.

Categories

Horizontally scrollable category chips let you filter products by type (e.g., "All", specific machinery categories). Tap a category to view only products in that category.

Featured Products

A horizontally scrollable carousel of highlighted products. Tap any product card to view its full details.

Recent Products

A vertical list of the most recently posted items below the featured section.

Pull to Refresh

Swipe down anywhere on the Home screen to refresh all data.

💡 Role Switcher: If you are a Tekeray (Lessee), the Home screen shows a tenant-specific dashboard view tailored to your needs.

5. Search & Filters

Searching

  1. Tap the Search bar on the Home screen or navigate to the Search tab.
  2. Type a keyword (e.g., "excavator", "loader", "truck").
  3. Press Enter or tap the search icon to see results.
  4. Tap the ✕ next to the search field to clear your query.

Filters

Tap the filter icon (⚙️) next to the search bar to access filters. Filters help you narrow down results by specific criteria such as price range, location, and category.

Search Results

Results are displayed as product cards. Tap any card to view the full product details page.

6. Categories

Categories organize all listings by type. You can access categories from the Home screen category chips or the dedicated Category screen. Each category displays a grid of products with images, titles, and prices.

7. Product Details

Tap on any product card to open its detailed view. The Product Details screen includes:

Image Gallery

  • A large hero image at the top with parallax scroll effect.
  • Tap the image to open a full-screen zoom view — use pinch gestures to zoom in and out.
  • A thumbnail gallery at the bottom lets you switch between all product images.

Product Information

Category Shows the product category and transaction type (Rent / Sale)
Title Name of the product listing
Location City, Sub-City, and Wereda where the item is located
Price Current price in the listed currency with Fixed / Negotiable indicator

Tabs: Description & Reviews

Description
Reviews

  • Reviews Tab: User reviews with star ratings, dates, and comments. Logged-in users can submit their own review with a star rating and text.

Actions

8. Cart & Checkout

  1. View your cart by tapping the Cart icon on the Home screen or navigating to the Cart screen.
  2. Review items in your cart — each shows the product image, title, price (in ETB), and quantity.
  3. Tap the trash icon (🗑️) to remove an item from the cart.
  4. Review the Total Amount displayed at the bottom.
  5. Tap "Checkout Now" to place your order.
  6. Upon success, you'll see a confirmation notification and be redirected to the Home screen.
ℹ️ Empty Cart: If your cart is empty, you'll see a prompt with a "Start Shopping" button that takes you back to the Home screen.

9. Posting a New Listing

Creating a new listing follows a 6-step guided wizard. Tap the "Post" tab in the bottom navigation to begin.

Step 1 — Type

  • Select the Service Type: Machinery or Vehicle
  • Select the Transaction Type: Rent or Sale

Step 2 — Category

  • Choose a product category from the list (e.g., Excavator, Loader, Crane, Truck).
  • Categories load dynamically based on the service type selected in Step 1.

Step 3 — Details

Title A short, descriptive name for your listing
Description A detailed description of the item's condition, features, etc.
Price The asking price — select the currency (ETB is default)
Fixed / Negotiable Indicate if the price is firm or open to offers
Brand Select the brand (e.g., Caterpillar, Komatsu) — depends on the chosen category
Specifications Fill in category-specific attributes like Model Year, Working Hours, Horse Power, etc.

Step 4 — Location

  • Select your City / Location from the horizontal chips.
  • Choose a Sub-City (appears after selecting a location).
  • Choose a Wereda (appears after selecting a sub-city).

Step 5 — Media

  • Product Photos: Tap the upload area to add photos of your item. You can add multiple images and remove any by tapping the ✕ icon.
  • Legal Documents: Upload images of ownership certificates, libre, or business license.
  • YouTube Video Link: Optionally paste a YouTube link showcasing your item.

Step 6 — Options (Posting Package)

  • Choose a Posting Package — each package has different visibility options and prices.
  • Select the package that fits your needs and tap "Post Item".
💡 Tip: You can go back to any previous step using the "Back" button. Your data is preserved across steps.

10. Jobs Module

The Jobs module connects machine operators with employers.

10.1 Finding & Applying for Jobs

  1. Tap the Jobs tab in the bottom navigation.
  2. Browse available job listings. Use the search bar to filter by keyword.
  3. Each job card shows the job title, description, location, and number of applicants.
  4. Tap a job card to view full details.
  5. Tap "Apply" to submit your application. A confirmation dialog will appear.
  6. If you've already applied, you'll see a "Already Applied" status instead.

Operator List

Employers can view a list of registered operators, including their profiles, experience, and machinery expertise.

Job Filters

Use the filters screen to narrow down job listings by location, category, or job type.

10.2 Posting a Job

  1. Navigate to your Profile and tap "Posted Jobs".
  2. Tap the "+" button to create a new job listing.
  3. Fill in the job title, description, location, and requirements.
  4. Submit the job posting.

10.3 Becoming an Operator

  1. Go to your Profile screen.
  2. Tap "Become an Operator" at the bottom of the page.
  3. Fill in the Operator Registration form with your skills, experience, and machinery expertise.
  4. Submit the form to register as an operator and start receiving job offers.
ℹ️ Already an Operator? If you're already registered, the button will read "Update Operator Profile", allowing you to update your details.

11. Supplier Requests

The Requests module allows users to post supply/demand requirements and browse existing ones.

Viewing Requests

  1. Tap the Requests tab in the bottom navigation.
  2. Browse the list of active requests. Each card shows the title, description, date, and requester info.
  3. Use the search bar to filter requests by keyword.
  4. Pull down to refresh the list.

Posting a Request

  1. On the Supplier Home screen, tap the "+" button to post a new request.
  2. Fill in the request details: title, description, quantity, and any specific requirements.
  3. Tap "Submit" to publish your request.

12. Messages & Chat

Viewing Messages

  1. Access your messages from the Home screen message icon or from the Messages screen.
  2. Your inbox shows all conversations with other users, sorted by most recent.
  3. Tap any conversation to open the full chat view.

Chatting

  • Type your message in the input field at the bottom.
  • Tap the send button to deliver your message.
  • Messages are delivered in real-time via the app's messaging system.

13. Notifications

Stay informed about activity related to your account:

  • Access notifications from the bell icon on the Home screen or from Profile → Notifications.
  • A red badge on the Notifications menu item shows your unread count.
  • Notifications include alerts for new messages, job applications, order updates, and more.

17. FAQ & Troubleshooting

Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?

Tap "Forgot Password?" on the login screen. Enter your phone number to receive an OTP, then set a new password.

Q: My account says "Not Verified." How do I verify?

When you try to sign in, a red banner with a "Verify" button will appear. Tap it to receive an OTP via SMS and complete verification.

Q: How do I change my role?

Your role is set during registration. To change your role, please contact RENR support via Profile → Help & Support.

Q: Why can't I see the Jobs, Post, or Requests tabs?

These features require you to be logged in. Please sign in first to access them.

Q: How do I become an operator?

Go to Profile → Become an Operator at the bottom of the profile screen and fill out the registration form.

Q: Can I edit my listing after posting?

Yes! Go to Profile → My Listings, find the listing, and tap to edit it. You can update the title, description, price, images, and other details.

Q: How do I contact a seller?

On the Product Details page, use the messaging feature or view the seller's contact info to start a conversation.

Q: What are posting packages?

When posting a new listing, the final step asks you to choose a posting package. Each package has different visibility levels and durations. Higher-tier packages give your listing more exposure.

Q: The app shows an error when I try to checkout.

Ensure you are logged in and have items in your cart. If the error persists, check your internet connection or contact support.
